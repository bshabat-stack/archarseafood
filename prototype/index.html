Since 1983 · Somerset, NJ

Four decades on Hamilton Street

Archar Seafood has been East Franklin's fish market for over 40 years. Ask around the neighborhood and you'll hear the same things: the fish is fresh, the prices are fair, and Michael behind the counter makes you feel like family.

Customers drive in from across the Tri-State Area for the blue claws and leave with dinner recommendations. That's not something you can ship in a box — you have to come by.

Honest sourcing

Wild-caught, and honest about the season

Most of what's in our case is wild-caught, and we don't pretend otherwise when it isn't. When something's out of season, we'll tell you — and point you to what's running instead.

Ask us where today's fish came from. If we can't answer, we shouldn't be selling it.
